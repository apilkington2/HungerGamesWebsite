Characters

Katniss Everdeen

16, and a tribute for the 74th hunger games. Is the main provider for her family because her dad had taught her to hunt before he died. She has one sister, Prim, who she would do anything for. She’s not super close with her mother, but does have a best friend, Gale Hawthorne, who she can tell anything to.

Peeta Mellark

A tribute for the 74th hunger games. He is the son of bakers, and helps work at the family bakery. He has had a little crush on Katniss since he was little, and wanted to be with her. He can’t hunt like Katniss, but he has an amazing talent with painting from painting the cakes at the bakery, that he can use to camouflage himself.

Gale Hawthorne

18 years old and is the best friend of Katniss Everdeen. Together the two of them would hunt and be providers for their two families. When Gale saw Peeta, and Katniss becoming romantic with each other he grew very jealous. He realized that he may have feelings for Katniss, but if Peeta is around he will never know if him and Katniss are meant to be together.

Primrose Everdeen

also known as Prim, she is a 12 year old girl that is also Katniss’s sister. She is small and kind of wimpy. She can’t hunt, but like her mother is very good with medical things, and taking care of people that are sick or injured. Also, Prim has a cat named Butterscotch that she loves very much.
